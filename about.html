7 Services Nursing Services is a member of AMA&Co.

It’s a nursing service providing company Giving budget friendly, quality services to all our valuable clients.
We are established in 2011 and registered company under the ministry of commerce and industry Qatar, we adhere to rigorous quality assurance standards and are committed to providing high quality services. 7 Services Nursing Services pride itself on offering exceptional and individualized professional services with certified licensed professionals in the fields of medical care, rehabilitation, asset management , home care management , clinic nurse . At 7 Services Nursing Services , we have professional , qualified, mixed genders, young, active, Arabic speaker with middle east experience medical staffs. Our primary focus is to serve the best of our client’s needs with a pledge of compassion, respect, and personal service.

Our Company Mission

Committed to advancing the mission of patient-centered medical excellence both, regionally and internationally, 7 Services Nursing Services operated in the State of Qatar for since 2011, growing on with more than 150 medical staffs.
The mission of 7 Services Nursing Services is to built on the foundations of knowledge, advanced technology, and compassion, our team continually strives to ensure that every patient’s healthcare journey is an excellent experience. Our operation team and support staff also work in a coordinated and compassionate manner, delivering the best care and outcome for all medical conditions. We offer skilled and team of professional staffs. We are constantly working to make our company the finest place to work in the sector. The workforce upholds the highest standards of ethics, integrity, and client-focused care, which defines our company culture.

Our Company Vision

Our vision to be leader in Qatar by assuring most satisfied unmatched services.7 Services Nursing Services medical service is setting a new standard in nursing service by creating an unmatched Culture of Care that drives industry-leading client satisfaction.
