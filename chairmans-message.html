Message from CEO

Within a short span of time, 7 Services Nursing Services has established itself as a leader in Qatar. Guided by principles and values from traditional Qatari culture in our daily tasks and work, we have achieved several milestones that have enabled 7 Services Nursing Services to become one of the top providers in the country.
At the same time as 7 Services Nursing Services , we will always be committed to treating patients with respect and dignity and providing them the highest-quality healthcare services in a professional and compassionate manner. In addition all our efforts , services and future expansion plans fall in line with the vision of HH Sheikh Tamim Bin Hamad Al-Thani, Emir of Qatar, and the objectives laid out in Qatar National Vision 2030.
